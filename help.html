Watt2Do

Understanding the battery

  • The battery on this side of the page shows how much energy you have.

  • The percentage underneath the battery represents how much energy you have.

  • To change the amount of energy, the field below can take in a number from 0 to 100.

  • When tasks are checked off as "finished", the energy within the bar will automatically decrease.

  • The bar will increase when "booster activities" are checked off.

Understanding Tasks

  • Adding a task requires the two fields to be filled: the title and energy.

  • The title of the assignment is what describes it.

  • The energy of an assignment represents how much energy of the battery it will take to complete.

  • After an assignment is added, you can either complete or delete it using the checkbox.
    • If the task is unable to be added, the energy field may not have been filled in correctly.

  • The completed tasks button marks the tasks as finished, getting rid of the energy in the bar.
    • If the task is unable to be finished, the tasks may exceed the amount of energy in the battery.

  • The deleted tasks button gets rid of tasks that will not be completed or were added by mistake.

Hover over an empty field or button to see what it says!


To go back, click the back button or the title at the top of the page.
