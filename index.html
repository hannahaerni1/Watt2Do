Welcome to Watt2Do, a to-do list that gives you the tasks you have the energy to complete.

To add a task item, put the name of the task in the first field and the amount of energy needed to complete the task in the second field. The energy will be shown as a percent, so be sure to make the number between 1 and 99.

Make sure to take care of yourself.
